Key Takeaways

Turnaround
Return au sommet
EBIT surged from €268M to €1,337M (+398%). The post-Yeezy crisis is fully behind the company, with operational profitability completely restored.
Margin
All-Time High
A 50.8% gross margin is a multi-year record, reflecting the brand's restored pricing power and successful move upmarket.
Bilan
Strong Deleveraging
Adjusted net debt fell 20% to €3.6Bn. The Debt/EBITDA ratio collapsed from 4.1× to 2.27×, significantly improving the risk profile.
Growth/div>
Organic Engine
Excluding Yeezy (~€200M), organic growth hit +13% on a currency-neutral basis — a strong signal of the brand's durable underlying momentum.
